Long Island Iced Tea

Category: Cocktail

Glass to Use: Collins Glass

Long Island Iced Tea Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 oz Vodka
  • 1/2 oz Rum
  • 1/2 oz Gin
  • 1/2 oz Tequila
  • 1 oz Sour Mix
  • Splash of Cola
Procedure:

Mix all ingredients together in a glass with ice. Garnish with a lemon wedge and serve.
